Savoj is brothers Jonathan and Andreas' first pub. We have always dreamed of what in our world is a perfect pizza. Light and fluffy, like cute little clouds, it's perfect! How do you make a perfect pizza then? Well, first you experiment with different batches of dough like a thousand times.
